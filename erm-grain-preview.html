What this is

You have spent decades earning the thing AI cannot replicate.

The relationships built over years. The decisions that never make it into a document. The judgment that only comes from being in the room.

AI is powerful. It is also not you. It does not know your clients. It did not sit in those meetings. It cannot tell you which part of your workflow is the real leverage point and which part just looks like one. You can.

Your experience is not the past. It is the starting point.

You bring a real workflow from your own practice. We map it. We audit it. We put AI inside the parts that belong to it. Then we look at what becomes possible when AI is carrying what it should.

The tools follow the goal. Not the other way around.

Who this is for

If you have used any AI tool to get real work done, even once, you are ready.

If you are starting from zero, AI 101 comes first. If you already use AI most days, you will go further here, not back over old ground. The only prerequisite is hands-on time with at least one tool, whether ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini.

The framework

See it. Sort it. Scale it.

Phase 01

See it

Foundation

Most experienced professionals carry their entire operating system inside their heads. This layer gets it onto a page, where it can actually be worked with. Messy is fine. Honest is required.

Phase 02

Sort it

The filter

Audit every piece of your Map with AI in mind. What should AI own? What requires genuine judgment and must stay human? What are you doing out of habit that stopped serving you years ago?

Phase 03

Scale it

Expansion

When AI owns what it should, something appears that was not visible before. Not just time saved. Time that now has somewhere better to go. This is the layer only experience can unlock.

The five sessions

Five weekly sessions. Light homework between each, always on your own work and never busywork.

01

See It

Make the invisible visible

Map the workflow only you know how to do. In class together, instructors model first, then you build your own. Messy is fine. Honesty is required.

02

See It, Deeper

Find what got edited out

The first map is never the real map. Small groups pressure test what got left out. Then you hand one small piece to AI and observe what happens.

03

Sort It

Decide what AI can and cannot own

Now you have real data. Run the audit: what should AI own, what stays human, where does agentic AI belong. Four sort tags, one heuristic, and working groups apply it to their own maps.

04

Sort It Applied + Scale It

What happened, and what it opens up

Report back on what happened when you handed work to AI. Then look at what the friction reveals, and what opens up once AI is doing its part. Scale is not just time saved. It is where that time goes.

05

Own It

Explain it and you own it

A working debrief, not a final exam. You walk a room of peers through your rebuilt workflow: what AI owns, what stayed human, what changed, and what is now possible.

You will leave with

A working map, not just notes.
